# External AI Video Prompts

Use `workmate-tiktok-keyvisual.png` as the image reference when the tool supports it.

## Runway / Pika Image-to-Video Prompt

Create a polished 15-second vertical 9:16 TikTok ad for WorkMate Australia, a free hospitality hiring platform in Sydney. Use the reference image as the visual style and first frame: warm modern Sydney cafe, smartphone app interface, friendly hospitality workers, trustworthy startup feel.

Motion: slow cinematic push-in toward the phone, subtle parallax between cafe background and smartphone, gentle UI highlights on job cards and message bubbles, smooth text overlay transitions. Keep the phone readable and centered. No chaotic camera movement.

On-screen text sequence:
0-2s: Still hiring through posts and group chats?
2-5s: Meet WorkMate Australia
5-8.5s: Post jobs. Browse staff. DM directly.
8.5-12s: Built for Sydney hospitality
12-15s: Start hiring for free today

Brand style: warm cream, coffee brown, white, soft gold. Clean sans-serif typography. High contrast captions. No watermark, no fake app store badge, no incorrect logo text, no extra slogans.

Voiceover:
Still trying to hire through posts and group chats? Meet WorkMate Australia. Post jobs, browse available staff, and message candidates directly. Built for cafes, restaurants, bars, working holiday makers, and international students in Sydney. Start hiring for free today.

## App Screen Demo Prompt

Create a 15-second vertical TikTok ad that looks like a polished mobile app demo for WorkMate Australia. Show the app after it has many real users: a busy jobs feed, staff profile cards, direct messages, and an employer applicant dashboard.

Important visual direction:
- Use warm cream, coffee brown, white, and soft gold.
- UI should look like a clean mobile app, not a website landing page.
- Show many Sydney hospitality jobs and candidates.
- Include realistic example data, but do not imply these are real named users.
- Keep all screens readable and high contrast.

Screen sequence:
0-3s: Home dashboard with stats: 128 staff profiles, 42 open jobs, 310 messages.
3-6s: Jobs feed with Barista, Sushi Roll Maker, Bartender listings.
6-9s: Staff profiles with WHV and international student candidates.
9-12s: Direct message conversation confirming a trial shift.
12-15s: Employer dashboard with applicants and hire/review buttons.

On-screen text:
When the app is active...
Jobs fill the feed.
Staff profiles are ready.
Hiring moves to DM.
WorkMate Australia - Start hiring for free.

Voiceover:
Imagine WorkMate once Sydney hospitality starts using it. Jobs fill the feed, staff profiles are ready, and hiring moves straight to DM. Cafes, restaurants, and bars can post jobs, browse candidates, and manage applicants in one place. WorkMate Australia. Start hiring for free.
